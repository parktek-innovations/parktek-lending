Frequently Asked Questions

What types of parking solutions do you offer?

We provide comprehensive parking management systems including:

  • Boom Barriers: Automated vehicle flow management
  • ANPR Systems: Advanced license plate recognition
  • POS Devices: Seamless payment processing
  • Web Dashboard: Real-time monitoring and analytics
  • Mobile Apps: User-friendly parking management

How does your system improve operational efficiency?

Our systems automate parking management, leading to:

  • Reduced manual intervention and labor costs
  • Enhanced traffic flow and reduced congestion
  • Real-time monitoring for informed decision-making
  • Automated billing and payment processing

Are your systems customizable?

Yes, our solutions are fully scalable and customizable for:

  • Small-scale facilities with limited parking spaces
  • Large-scale facilities with high traffic volumes
  • Specialized environments like hospitals, malls, and airports
  • Residential complexes and corporate campuses

What payment options are supported?

Our POS devices support multiple payment methods:

  • Cash payments with automated change dispensing
  • Credit/Debit cards from all major networks
  • Mobile wallets and UPI payments
  • FASTag and prepaid cards

What kind of support do you provide?

We offer comprehensive support services:

  • Professional installation and commissioning
  • 24/7 technical support
  • Preventive and periodic maintenance
  • Staff training and onboarding
  • Regular system updates and upgrades
